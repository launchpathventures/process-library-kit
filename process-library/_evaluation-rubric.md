## Scoring Instructions

1. Read the complete output before scoring any dimension
2. Score each dimension independently — don't let one strong area inflate others
3. For weighted dimensions, multiply raw score by weight
4. Sum all weighted scores for the total
5. Compare to passing and target scores:
   - **Below passing**: Output needs revision before use
   - **Between passing and target**: Usable but could improve
   - **At or above target**: Meets the quality bar

## Gold Standard Comparison Protocol

After scoring, compare against the gold standard:

1. **Structure**: Does output have the same sections? Missing sections = score 1 on relevant dimensions
2. **Specificity**: Find the most concrete claim in both gold standard and output — are they at the same level?
3. **Gap narrative**: For any dimension below 4, write one sentence on what the gold standard does better
